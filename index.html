About Us

We envision a community where families and youth are strong and resilient

Our Vision

We envision a community where families and youth are strong and resilient and have access to the resources and support they need to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide access to sports, recreation, mentorship, and programs that promote personal growth, academic achievement, and community engagement. We also offer education, counseling, and support services for Somali immigrants and refugees in need.

Our Mandate

As a charitable organization, our mandate is to provide education, counseling, and other support services to Somali immigrants and refugees in need. We offer language instruction, employment training, job search programs, translation services, and information programs on Canadian culture and life.
